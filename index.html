    Biography.

     

    My name is Rafael Miranda, and I'm a Interaction Designer. I studied at PUC-RIO, where I got my bachelor's degree in Digital Media Design.

    Six years ago, I wished to do everything related to Design, except Webdesign, so I entered university to work with animation.

    There, I was forced by destiny – thanks by the way Mr.Destiny – to enter in a project of web, and then, my world changed. Now my life is made of UX Sketchs and protypes, a PSD with tons of layers and my black code editor to make me happy everyday.

    I started to work with Web in the end of the first year of the university as an intern and since my graduation I work in a Mobile Company, called Mobicare, creating with mobile websites and mobile applications. There, I worked with the biggest company of communication in Brazil, doing several apps, from technical services to consumer services.

    I think i'm special cause i'm a Designer and a want-to-be Front-End Developer. But why, you may ask? Cause, as a Designer, I was trained to think in a different way, and I was trained to push limits. So, my ideas are very clear and objective in my mind, but sometimes, hard to explain to others, so nobody is better to code it, than myself.
